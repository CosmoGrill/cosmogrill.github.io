  • the why

    The instructors crew behind Ripple X has long been frustrated with the dated curricilums of traditional colleges. We took part with the leading technology schools that started emerging in the early 2010's, but we were missing the depth and impact of a good educational experience. 

    So we set to solve these programs by bringing a new educational experience to the forefront. Ripple X was founded with with the idea that courses should go beyond surface level.  Our company is owned and operated by practitioners and educators, so that the content and you come first.

  • Class design

    The Ripple X content is designed from scratch in  a selective fashion. We include strategies and tactics that stand the rapid change in the business and technology landscapes. We exclude the obvious laundry lists of items commonly found in technology classes.

    Our classes are optimized for impact. We look to evoke unconventional, out-of-the-box type of thinking, necessary for today's reality. We strive to leave you with takeaways that make a big impact when you are back to work.

    And new insights are best attained in an immersive, interactive experience. We engage all your sense with wine and a meal, and leave enough room for the classmates to interact and co-create.

  • our crew

    Our instructors crew is made of the most original and accomplished practitioners of their fields. As thought leaders, they offer content that you won't find elsewhere. As experienced professionals, they will be able to give advice based on real world experience.
